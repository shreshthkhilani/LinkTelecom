About



Link Telecom Pvt. Ltd. is a value added distribution and market development company-one of the largest Tier-1 distribution houses of global IT and Telecom brands in Eastern and North Eastern India. We are the exclusive distributors for HP, HTC and LG in West Bengal, Sikkim, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha and the North East. At Link Telecom, we believe in the sustained growth of all stakeholders, as each resource is critical to the profitability of the organization. By leveraging our unique market insights, together with an unmatched distribution network and marketing support services, we enable our partners to succeed in today's dynamic environment. We are a value added distribution organization, facilitating brand building, geographic expansion, market development and distribution in our geography for our brand partners. Without sacrificing the ideologies of the associated brands, we have been creating high value & brand equity for our partners. We practice this philosophy throughout the organization which allows us to transcend geographical boundaries and form mutually beneficial relationships.
