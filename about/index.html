A Message From Our Board:


What makes Link Telecom unique? Simple. It’s our relationships.

With more than 110 years of industry experience our board understands the value of maintaining transparent and ethical relationships with business partners. In a marketplace with hundreds of new entrants every year, both telecommunications and information technology are undergoing tectonic shifts in India.

As fresh challengers keep nudging at the fundamentals of the status quo, an ecosystem of retailers, distributors, national distributors and principal companies working together as one cohesive unit, can face obstacles head on to fuel business and consumer growth.

It is our modus operandi to successfully manage relationships with the hundreds of distributor partners and the thousands of retailers we do business with. To do this we have a flat manpower structure along with an open door culture; this allows us to maintain the work environment of a small family.

Respected and sought after, Link Telecom is excited and proud to be one of India’s largest distribution houses and it our joy to lead this company into the future.

 
 
 

Our Values:


HONESTY
We strive to set the gold standard in honesty and ethics, which at times means choosing between what is right and what is easy. We do this, at all times. Every time.

COMMITMENT
Principals, partners, and employees recognize our brand name through the our unique way of working. We believe in honouring all our commitments.



KNOWLEDGE
We analyze existing market trends and translate them into opportunities for our partners and ourselves. This allows us to have an informed point of view at all times.

EFFICIENCY
A flexible organization with the latest technology allows us to maximize efficiency for all players in our ecosystem.
