A Message From Our Board:


What makes Link Telecom unique? Simple. It’s our relationships.

With more than 110 years of industry experience our board understands the value of maintaining transparent and ethical relationships with business partners. In a marketplace with hundreds of new entrants every year, both telecommunications and information technology are undergoing tectonic shifts in India. Leveraging relationships allows us to gain in-depth market knowledge; as well as, create value for our distributor partners, principal companies and retailers.

Growth Strategy:

The biggest issue scaling up in a distribution business is, sustaining the relationships we maintain with the hundreds of distributor partners and thousands of retailers we do business on an everyday basis with. To do this we have created a revolutionary flat manpower structure coupled with an open door culture. This makes our senior management very informed about the nuances of business across India’s varied geography whilst maintaining the culture of a small family.

New Horizons:

Link Telecom is perfectly positioned to deal with all the disruptions that both IT and Telecom industries are currently facing. We are able to cater to the needs of both existing stalwarts and new revolutionary market entrants vying to make a foothold in one of the largest markets in the world. Our passion and advice to our principal companies have allowed them to look beyond the existing price war and become truly innovative organizations with new revenue streams and strong brand names.

As fresh challengers keep nudging at the fundamentals of the status quo, an ecosystem of retailers, distributors, national distributors and principal companies working together as one cohesive unit, can face obstacles head on to fuel business and consumer growth.

Respected and sought after, Link Telecom is excited and proud to be one of India’s largest distribution houses and it our joy to lead this company into the future.

 
 
 

Our Values:


Honesty

We value open, direct and transparent communication both internally and externally. We strive to set the gold standard in honesty and ethics, which at times means choosing between what is right and what is easy. We do this, at all times. Every time.

Commitment

Creating long lasting relationships with brands is an arduous task, which we do not take lightly. Principals, partners, and employees recognize our brand name through the value we have created in the market place.

Knowledge

Knowledge is acquired through being passionate about solving intricate problems and following patterns that shape the next generation of distribution and technology. This allows us to have an informed point of view at all times, as we analyze existing market trends and translate them into opportunities for our partners and ourselves.

Efficiency

A flexible organization with the latest technology allows us to maximize efficiency for all players in our ecosystem. Our consistency in performing at the highest level possible coupled with our expanding resource base has allowed us to nurture, develop and transform brands.
