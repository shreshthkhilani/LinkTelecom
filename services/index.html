Market Development


Market development is an art, which has to be honed and refined. At Link Telecom, we are proud to say that we have repeated this to a point of perfection.

In 2002, we became city distributors in Kolkata for Nokia. Through this business we successfully created the platform for telecom in this city. We rapidly expanded our width of distribution and nurtured several of the largest retailers in the city.

In 2008, we moved from city distributors to national distributors selling both Samsung phones and HP laptops across East India. Samsung at that point was not yet a major player, but we were able to successfully replicate our success with Nokia and apply it in on a large scale. Soon we were dealing with 2 products that were market leaders in their respective segments.

Today, Link telecom, is working with several major brands which are all uniquely positioned in different product categories.
